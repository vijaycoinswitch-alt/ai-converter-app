Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

1. File Security and Storage

Your files belong to you. When you upload a file to Convertify for conversion, it is transmitted securely via 256-bit TLS encryption. Files are processed entirely automatically by our servers. No human ever accesses or views your content. All uploaded and converted files are permanently and automatically deleted from our storage systems exactly 2 hours after your task is completed.

2. Data Collection

We collect minimal personal data. If you create an account, we store your username and hashed password to authenticate you. If you upgrade to Pro, payment processing is handled strictly by third-party processors (like Stripe); we never store full credit card details on our own servers.

3. Cookies

We use minimal cookies necessary to keep your session authenticated securely and store your Dark/Light mode preferences.
